Privacy Policy

Location powers the forecast, favorites stay on your device, and weather data comes from third-party services.

Privacy Terms

Last updated: April 28, 2026

JackCast is a weather app that shows current conditions, forecasts, air quality, and location-based weather artwork. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled.

Location

JackCast may ask your browser for permission to access your device location. If you allow it, your latitude and longitude are used to load weather and air-quality data for your current area. If you decline, the app falls back to a default location and you can search for a city manually.

Weather Data Providers

JackCast requests weather, air-quality, and city search data from Open-Meteo services. These requests may include coordinates or city search text so the provider can return relevant forecast data. Open-Meteo processes those requests under its own terms and privacy practices.

Local Storage

Saved favorite locations are stored locally in your browser on your device. JackCast does not require an account and does not sync favorites to a JackCast server.

Personal Data

JackCast does not sell personal data, does not include an account system, and does not intentionally collect contact information, names, or payment details.

Installed App

When installed as a Progressive Web App, JackCast may cache app files and recent weather responses so the app can load quickly and provide limited offline support.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app publisher through codingnoodles.com.
